Privacy & Security

Last Updated: August 20, 2025

Welcome to our website. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your experience is secure. This page outlines our privacy practices in the most transparent way possible.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not use any cookies, trackers, or analytics services that collect personal data. We do not require users to log in, register, or provide any information to access our content. We only track which pages or posts were clicked anonymously. This feature is delivered by our hosting provider.

Your Anonymity

When you visit our website, your anonymity is respected. We do not know who you are, and we do not store any personal information about you.

Log Files

Like most websites, our web server may create standard log files. These files typically include your IP address, browser type, and pages visited. This information is used for technical purposes, such as maintaining the security and stability of our server, and is not linked to any personal identifying information. We do not use this data to track or identify individual users.

External Links

Our website may contain links to other sites. Please be aware that we are not responsible for the privacy practices of these other sites. We encourage you to read the privacy statements of any other site that collects personal information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy and Security page from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
